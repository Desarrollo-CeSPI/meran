Choose Multiple Days on a Flat Calendar

  1. This demo uses theme yellow.
  2. This is a flat or "in the page" calendar. It is displayed when the page loads rather than when the user clicks on a button.
  3. You can now choose multiple days in the calendar.
  4. You can click on a chosen date to remove it from the list of chosen dates.
  5. The following dates have been programmatically chosen
    • 2004 April 25
    • 2004 April 18
    • 2004 April 22
    • 2004 April 05
    You can uncheck these.
  6. Click on Submit. The dates are now displayed in the text area.
